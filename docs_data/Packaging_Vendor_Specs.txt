### CUSTOM PACKAGING SPECIFICATIONS

**1. Standard Packaging Boxes**  
- **Dimensions:** Custom sizes available upon request.  
- **Material:** High-quality corrugated cardboard.  
- **Inserts:** Custom cardboard inserts are required to secure products during transit.  
- **Sealing:** Minimum of three (3) branded packing tape strips required per box to ensure secure closure.  
- **Printing:** Full-color printing available on packaging for branding purposes.  
- **Durability Rating:** Designed to withstand standard shipping and handling conditions.

**2. Eco-Friendly Packaging Line**  
- **Material:** Compostable bio-wrap that is environmentally sustainable.  
- **Functionality:** The bio-wrap tears easily for user convenience.  
- **Closure Method:** Requires hand-tying with twine, presenting a design challenge for operational efficiency.  
- **Sustainability Certification:** Complies with ASTM D6400 standards for compostability.  
- **Customization:** Available in various sizes and colors upon request.
