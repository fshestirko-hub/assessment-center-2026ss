**Employee Pulse Survey Summary Report**  
**Date:** October 15, 2023  
**Prepared by:** Maria Thompson, HR Generalist  

**Overview:**  
We recently conducted a pulse survey among our warehouse employees to gather feedback on their experiences and identify areas for improvement. The survey received a commendable participation rate of 85%, demonstrating our workforce's commitment to making Zenith Active Solutions a great place to work. Below is a summary of the feedback received, highlighting both positive aspects and opportunities for enhancement.

**Positive Feedback:**

1. **Work Environment:**  
   Many employees expressed appreciation for the overall team atmosphere in the warehouse. The camaraderie and support among colleagues were frequently mentioned, indicating a strong sense of community.

2. **Snacks and Breaks:**  
   A number of employees noted that the recent introduction of healthier snack options has been a welcome change. Suggestions for expanding the variety of snacks available were also popular, and we will explore options to include more choices in the coming weeks.

3. **Parking Facilities:**  
   Employees have requested improved parking arrangements. While the current parking lot is convenient, there is interest in optimizing space to alleviate congestion during peak hours. We are considering options for additional signage and designated parking areas to enhance accessibility.

**Areas for Improvement:**

1. **Work-Life Balance:**  
   While many employees appreciate the flexible scheduling options, there is a notable concern regarding work-life balance. Several employees highlighted the need for more predictable scheduling, especially given the consecutive mandatory weekend shifts over the last year. It’s important to acknowledge that this has contributed to feelings of burnout on the floor. We are committed to addressing these concerns and will evaluate scheduling practices to better support employee well-being.

2. **Training and Development:**  
   Employees expressed interest in additional training opportunities to develop their skills further. There is a strong desire for growth within the company, and we will work on implementing more robust training programs to foster professional development.

3. **Communication:**  
   Some feedback pointed to the need for improved communication from management regarding company updates and operational changes. We are dedicated to enhancing transparency and will introduce regular team meetings to ensure everyone is informed and involved in the company's journey.

**Conclusion:**  
While there are challenges to address, the survey results reflect a committed workforce eager to see improvements. We value the feedback from our warehouse team and are dedicated to making Zenith Active Solutions a supportive and thriving workplace. As we move forward, we will prioritize the identified areas for improvement while fostering the positive aspects that make our company culture strong.

Thank you for your continued dedication and hard work!

**Next Steps:**  
We will share the findings with management and develop a concrete action plan to address employee concerns over the coming weeks. We encourage ongoing dialogue and invite any additional feedback through our open-door policy.
