SUBJECT: Q3 All-Hands Recap — Thank You, Team Zenith 💪

Team,

A mentor once told me: "The measure of a company is not how it performs when everything goes right — it's how it shows up when things get hard." I've thought about that a lot this quarter.

And I want to say, simply: I am proud of this team.

─────────────────────────────────────
WHAT WE ACCOMPLISHED THIS QUARTER
─────────────────────────────────────

Q3 marked another quarter of record-breaking revenue for Zenith Active Solutions. We shipped more units, served more customers, and expanded our reach into new channels than at any point in our company's history. That does not happen without each and every one of you showing up and doing the work.

Our DTC channel continues to outperform. Our influencer and creator partnerships are generating awareness at a scale that would have been unimaginable when we launched. And our product catalog — now over 200 SKUs — is a testament to how far we've come from the scrappy team that started this journey with a single hero product and a belief that fitness should be accessible to everyone.

This is just the beginning.

─────────────────────────────────────
THE ROAD AHEAD
─────────────────────────────────────

Are there headwinds? Yes. Every growing company faces them. But I want to be clear: headwinds are not stop signs. They are invitations to get smarter, leaner, and more intentional about how we operate. And that is exactly what we are doing.

We are leaning into our strengths. We are doubling down on the things that make Zenith, Zenith. We are asking hard questions and building better answers. Exciting times are ahead — and I mean that genuinely, not as a platitude, but as someone who sees the work happening across every department and knows what we are capable of.

The next two quarters will require focus. But focus is something this team has never lacked.

─────────────────────────────────────
OUR PEOPLE ARE OUR BIGGEST ASSET
─────────────────────────────────────

I want to take a moment to recognize a few things that happened behind the scenes this quarter that don't always get celebrated:

The warehouse team handled a 34% spike in order volume in September with minimal disruption. The ops team rebuilt our carrier routing logic from scratch in under three weeks. And the customer experience team maintained a 4.2/5 satisfaction score during our busiest month on record.

None of that is accidental. That is culture. That is what "Bold. Relentless. Real." actually looks like in practice.

If you haven't seen our updated values wall in the break room — go look at it. It's a good reminder of who we are and what we're building together.

The best chapter of this company's story hasn't been written yet. We write it together.
