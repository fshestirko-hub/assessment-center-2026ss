**Zenith Active Solutions Brand Guidelines Summary**

*Date: October 12, 2023*  
*Prepared by: Jamie Sinclair, Creative Director*  

---

**Hey Zenith Fam!**  

Welcome to the vibe check of our brand! We're not just here to sell fitness apparel; we're here to redefine the fitness game! Buckle up, because we've laid down some major guidelines to keep our aesthetic and voice as fresh as a post-workout smoothie. 

**Brand Voice: Disruptive, Loud, and Unapologetic**  
Our voice is like the beat drop in your favorite workout playlist – it’s got to hit hard and leave an impression! We’re all about being bold, edgy, and a little irreverent. Embrace the unconventional, shout out our message from the rooftops, and never back down from being authentic. Let’s keep the energy high and the attitude even higher! Every communication piece should resonate with our audience, making them feel seen, heard, and pumped to join the Zenith revolution.

**Visuals: Neon Dreams & Flashy Feels**  
Picture this: Neon colors that pop harder than your latest PR and high-contrast flash photography that screams “LOOK AT ME!” We’re all about creating visuals that are as eye-catching as they are engaging. Think vibrant pinks, electric blues, and zesty yellows that demand attention. Our imagery should be dynamic and full of life, capturing movement and the essence of our active community. Let’s make sure every shot feels like a burst of energy – we want our audience to feel the sweat, the passion, and the fun!

**Core Strategy: Viral TikTok & Influencer Power**  
Here’s where we get real: Our primary growth lever is all about going viral on TikTok. We want every post, every challenge, every trend to have our brand at its core and to be the talk of the ‘For You’ page. Partnering with massive fitness influencers? YES, and at all costs! We need to collaborate with those who have the megaphone to amplify our message and reach the fitness fanatics where they live. Let’s embrace the chaos of social media and turn every scroll into a potential sale, with our brand being the star of the show.

---

That's the scoop, Zenith squad! Let’s keep the vibes and energy high as we push the boundaries of fitness apparel and take the world by storm. Keep it loud, keep it bright, and let’s make magic happen!

Stay rad,
