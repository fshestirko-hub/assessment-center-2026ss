I hope this message finds you well. As we wrap up another week, I wanted to share the top reasons our customers reached out to support this week. It’s crucial we stay aligned on these issues as they directly impact our customer satisfaction and, ultimately, our bottom line.

This rush may be contributing to increased mis-picks, frustrating our customers and complicating returns. We need to address this immediately to avoid further dissatisfaction.

I would appreciate your attention to these matters in our upcoming operational reviews. We cannot afford to overlook the direct correlation between these issues and our dwindling customer loyalty.
